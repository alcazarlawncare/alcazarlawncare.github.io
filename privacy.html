Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Alcazar Lawncare Services ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or contact us through it.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide. When you submit our quote or contact form, we collect the details you give us — typically your name, phone number, email address, and the contents of your message.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we and our service providers may collect limited technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages viewed, through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your request and provide a quote or service;
  • To schedule and deliver the services you ask about;
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website;

  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to understand traffic and usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect some site functionality.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with trusted service providers that help us operate the site (such as our form-handling and analytics providers), and where required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect the information you submit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain the information you submit only as long as needed to respond to your request and for legitimate business or legal purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information (including under the CCPA/CPRA or GDPR). To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to review their policies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our site is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
